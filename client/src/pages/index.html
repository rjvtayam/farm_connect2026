Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about Farm Connect and our services

Farm Connect is a centralized agri-fishery information system for municipalities in Laguna. It is used by Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO) staff, Encoders, Verifiers, and System Administrators to manage farmer and fisherfolk registrations, beneficiary records, and government intervention tracking.

Beneficiary registration is handled through authorized MAO staff and Encoders at your local Municipal Agriculture Office. Visit your nearest MAO office with your valid IDs and supporting documents. The Encoder will input your information into Farm Connect for processing and verification.

Farm Connect currently supports enrollment for RSBSA (Registry System for Basic Sectors in Agriculture), FishR (Fisherfolk Registration), BoatR (Boat Registration), and NCFRS (National Coordination Framework for Relief and Sustainable Development). Additional programs may be added as needed.

We take data privacy seriously. Farm Connect implements municipality-level data isolation, meaning your municipality's data is completely separate from other LGUs. The system uses two-factor authentication, encrypted connections, and role-based access control to ensure only authorized personnel can access records.

Yes! Farm Connect is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Staff in the field can use NFC-enabled devices for quick authentication and data entry even in areas with limited connectivity, thanks to our offline-ready functionality.

Registrations follow a structured pipeline: Encoder creates the record → Verifier reviews and validates → MAO Staff gives final approval. Each step is fully tracked with an audit trail for transparency and accountability.

Yes, the system includes GIS-based farm parcel mapping that allows geo-tagging of agricultural land, fishing zones, and barangay-level data visualization. This helps MAO staff monitor land use, plan interventions, and visualize beneficiary distribution across the municipality.

For technical support, you can contact our team through the Contact Us section below, email us at support@lspu.edu.ph, or call the helpdesk at (049) 557-1142. Our support team is available during office hours, Monday to Friday.
